Our Journey So Far

  • Ph.D. Entrance Exams

    Structured Guidance

    Providing structured guidance for various universities’ Ph.D. research admission tests, including syllabus coverage, research methodology, and interview preparation.

  • UGC NET-JRF

    Comprehensive Coaching

    Complete preparation for Paper I & II with regular mock tests, updated study material, and expert mentoring to help candidates qualify for lectureship and Junior Research Fellowship.

  • State Eligibility Tests (SET/SLET)

    Targeted Training

    Focused coaching for state-level eligibility exams, covering state-specific patterns, past papers, and exam strategies.

    Exclusive Batches

    Specialized coaching for JKPSC (KAS) aspirants, with mains-qualified faculty, structured study material, interview guidance, and full exam preparation support.

Students

Join our programs to prepare for Ph.D. Entrance Exams, UGC NET-JRF, and SET/SLET. Gain expert guidance on research methodology, exam preparation, mock tests, and personalized mentorship. Participate in workshops, webinars, and research projects to enhance academic skills and career readiness.
