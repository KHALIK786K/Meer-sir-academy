Frequently Asked Questions

Why Choose Meer Sir Academy?
Meer Sir Academy (MSA) is known for its experienced faculty, personal attention, and result-oriented teaching methods. We focus on conceptual clarity and provide comprehensive preparation for school, entrance, and professional exams.
What courses and classes do you offer?
We offer coaching for Classes 1st to 12th (all streams), Undergraduate (BA, B.Sc, B.Com, BBA, B.Ed), Postgraduate (MA, M.Sc, M.Com, MBA) courses, and entrance/competitive exams including CUET, NEET, AMU, UPSC, CTET, DSSSB, NET/JRF, and more.
How can I take admission?
Admissions can be taken directly at our Batla House, Jamia Nagar branch. You can also contact us through phone or WhatsApp for details. Seats are limited, and early registration ensures timely batch allocation.
What is the fee structure?
Our fees vary depending on the course and class. Regular classes start from ₹800 per month, and entrance courses begin from ₹15,000. We also provide discounts and concessions for meritorious and financially weak students.
Do you provide demo classes?
Yes, we offer one free demo class for new students so they can experience our teaching style and interact with faculty before admission.
Do you have separate batches for boys and girls?
Yes, we maintain separate batches for boys and girls in specific classes to ensure a comfortable learning environment.
Is there any online class facility?
Yes, we provide both online and offline classes. Students can attend through our virtual classroom portal if they are unable to join physically.
