Frequently Asked Questions

What courses does Meer Sir Academy offer?
Meer Sir Academy offers regular and entrance coaching for Classes 6th, 9th, and 11th (Arts, Science, Commerce), undergraduate and postgraduate courses, as well as competitive exams like NET/JRF, SET, UPSC, DSSSB, CTET, CUET, and NEET.
What is the fee structure?
Fees vary by class and course. For example, regular classes for 1st–4th are ₹1000 (₹800 after concession), 9th–10th are ₹2500 (₹2000 after concession), BA/BSc/BCom ₹3000 (₹2500 after concession), and postgraduate programs like MA/MSc/MCom ₹5000 (₹3500 after concession). Entrance courses have separate fees depending on the program.
Who are the faculty members?
Our experienced faculty includes Meer Sir (Founder & Head Mentor), Isha Zehra (Managing Director), Suhail Sir (Senior Science Faculty), and Khan Sir (Mathematics Faculty). All faculty focus on conceptual clarity, personalized mentoring, and exam-focused strategies.
Where is the academy located?
Meer Sir Academy is located at Batla House, Jamia Nagar, New Delhi.
How can I enroll in a course?
You can enroll by contacting the academy directly via phone or email at meersir23@gmail.com. We will guide you through course selection and registration.
