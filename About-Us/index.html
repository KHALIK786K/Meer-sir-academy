MEER SIR ACADEMY

Meer Sir Academy (MSA) is the best coaching institute located at Batla House, Jamia Nagar, New Delhi, recognized for delivering quality education and consistent results. We offer separate regular and entrance classes for school students of Classes 6th, 9th, and 11th (Arts, Science, and Commerce streams), along with comprehensive coaching for Undergraduate Programs (BA, BA LLB, BBA, B.Sc, B.Com, BFA, B.Ed, D.El.Ed etc.) and Postgraduate Courses (MA, MBA, M.Sc, M.Com, MFA, M.Ed). We also provide specialized coaching for competitive and professional examinations such as NET/JRF, SET, UPSC, UPPSC, DSSSB, CTET, CUET, NEET, and AMU entrance tests.

The academy covers an extensive range of subjects across Humanities, Social Sciences, Sciences, and Professional Studies, including Economics, Sociology, Psychology, Political Science, History, Geography, Education, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Biotechnology, Mathematics, Law, Commerce, Business Administration, Islamic Studies, English, Urdu, Persian, and Arabic. Focused academic assistance is also provided for NIOS, IGNOU, and JMI CDOL students.
